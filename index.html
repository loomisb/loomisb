About Me

Front End Developer

My name is Blaine Loomis and I am a Front End Developer based in Phoenix, Arizona.

I specialize in HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and ReactJS.

Beginning at a young age, I have always been passionate about technology and enjoy using my creativity to develop interactive and user friendly, websites and applications. After leaving the United States Marine Corps in 2014, I attended Northern Arizona University where I gained foundational knowledge around Business Administration and Management. While attending school, I began growing my career in tech by driving digital innovation for small businesses and spearheading implementation of technology solutions that saved small businesses time and money. I began learning to code by attending a Web Developer Bootcamp on Udemy and completing self-paced courses on freeCodeCamp.

My goal is to use my knowledge and expertise to help your Team with Front End Web and Application development and continue learning along the way.
